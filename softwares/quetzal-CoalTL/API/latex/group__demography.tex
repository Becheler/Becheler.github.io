\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Template classes for building complex demographies for further conditional coalescence simulation. 

Defines template classes for storage and retrieval of demographic quantities of interest such as populations size distribution across space and time, or demographic flows between demes at all times.

These quantities are used in coalescence models for defining coalescence probability or backward migration probabilities of gene copies.
